# Ownership

Open Local Digital Twin is owned and maintained by Polisplexity Ltd, a United
Kingdom company building local digital twin and civic intelligence technology
for cities, public-interest organizations, and institutional partners.

Hadox Research Labs contributes research, methodology, civic AI, and local
digital twin standards work. Hadox Research Labs operates as a research and
technology vertical associated with Nodo Guanajuato A.C.

Nodo Guanajuato A.C. contributes public-interest, nonprofit, civic technology,
research, and ecosystem-building context in Mexico and Latin America.

## Public-Good Boundary

The public repository contains the open runtime, documentation, data model,
standards architecture, and reusable public-data methodology.

The following are outside the public release:

- private Polisplexity deployments;
- customer or partner data;
- private city data;
- credentials, tokens, keys, or server configuration;
- private commercial support agreements;
- internal project management notes;
- private research or proposal work not intentionally released.
